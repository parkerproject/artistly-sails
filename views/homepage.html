Our Story

What is our story? Simply put, it’s a story of the underdog. It’s a story of how we are trying to fight for more awareness for the small, independent artists out there that are going up against the goliaths.

It’s how we’re building something that can get more of these indie artist’s work out there and known. Now that you know why, it’s time to move on to how. We’re building something that will let you know about the latest events happening in your area, and then zoom in and find the artists behind the works at the events.

If you like there work, then feel free to tip them and help us win one more fight. In a couple words, we’re trying to create huge indie artists. Yes, that is an oxymoron. We’ll create a whole new way of discovering these indie artists, helping them out and pushing them up to bigger heights. We’re creating the future of indie art.
